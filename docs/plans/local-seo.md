# Local SEO Guidelines

## NAP Rules
- Use the exact same business name, address, and phone everywhere.
- Keep formatting identical across site, schema, GBP, and directories.

## Google Business Profile (GBP)
- Primary category: Painter
- Secondary categories: Commercial Painter, Painting Contractor
- Complete services, hours, and service areas
- Post weekly updates and add new photos monthly
- Respond to reviews within 24–48 hours

## Citations (Quality First)
- Google Business Profile
- Bing Places
- Apple Maps
- Yelp
- HomeStars
- BBB (if applicable)

## Local Content Signals
- Edmonton listed first on all service-area mentions
- Add neighborhood mentions sparingly (2–4 per page)
- Use local project photos whenever possible
