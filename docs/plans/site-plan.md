# NCS Painting Site Plan (Landing Page Only)

## Purpose

Define the landing page structure and content sections needed to hit the SEO goals:
- Rank for “residential painting companies in Edmonton” and related local intent keywords.
- Increase qualified leads via calls and quote requests.

## SEO Goals (Summary)
- **Primary**: Rank higher for residential painting searches in Edmonton.
- **Secondary**: Increase qualified leads (calls + quote requests).
- **Supporting**: Improve visibility for St. Albert, Sherwood Park, and Fort Saskatchewan while maintaining strong local trust and performance.

## Strategy Summary (How We Achieve the Goals)
- **Local intent targeting**: Dedicated service + location pages with Edmonton as the primary focus.
- **Internal linking**: Home → Services → Projects → Contact loop to build relevance and authority.
- **Proof-first content**: Projects, testimonials, FAQs, and process sections to raise trust and conversions.
- **NAP consistency**: Identical business name/address/phone across pages, schema, and GBP.
- **Keyword mapping control**: Use mapped variations to avoid cannibalization and thin pages.
- **On-page SEO basics**: Title/H1 alignment, descriptive alt text, schema, and CTA placement.
- **Performance focus**: Image budgets + Core Web Vitals targets to protect rankings.
- **Conversion tracking**: CTA, call, and form events to measure outcomes.

## Landing Page (Home)

**Primary intent:** “Painters / house painters Edmonton”  
**Secondary intent:** “interior painting Edmonton”, “exterior painting Edmonton”, “residential painters Edmonton”, local neighborhoods  
**Primary CTA:** Free quote / call now  

### Landing Page Content Sections (SEO‑Required)

1. **Hero (Above the fold)**
   - H1 targeting “Painters / House Painters Edmonton”
   - Short value proposition + trust signals (30 years, locally owned, insured if verified)
   - Primary CTA (quote) + secondary CTA (call)

2. **Service Overview (Core Residential Services)**
   - Clear cards for Interior + Exterior
   - Short supporting copy with Edmonton modifier
   - Links to service pages (future)

3. **Why Choose NCS Painting**
   - Differentiators: craftsmanship, clean prep, transparent process, warranty if verified
   - Local trust cues (family-owned, 30+ years)

4. **Exterior Painting Highlight**
   - Surface prep emphasis, weather durability, curb appeal
   - Visual proof (project image)

5. **Interior Painting Highlight**
   - Trim/ceilings/walls, dust control, smooth finish
   - Visual proof (project image)

6. **Process Overview**
   - Step-by-step: estimate → prep → paint → walkthrough
   - Reinforces professionalism and reliability

7. **Proof / Recent Projects**
   - Before/after gallery or recent jobs
   - Local photo proof for Edmonton + surrounding areas

8. **Testimonials / Reviews**
   - Short, credible quotes
   - Only include ratings if verifiable and also present in schema

9. **Service Areas**
   - Edmonton first, then Sherwood Park, St. Albert, Fort Saskatchewan
   - Mentions help with localized relevance

10. **FAQ (Residential Painting Edmonton)**
    - Pricing range, timing, prep, paint selection
    - Supports long-tail query capture

11. **Contact / Conversion CTA**
    - Quote form + tap-to-call
    - Reminder of response time and free estimate

12. **Trust Bar (Optional but Recommended)**
    - Insurance badge, years in business, local ownership
    - Keep claims verifiable

## Notes / Guardrails

- **One primary intent per page** to avoid keyword cannibalization.
- **NAP consistency** across footer/contact/schema.
- **Unique, non‑templated copy** for each section (avoid thin content).
- **Anchor text** should include “Edmonton” where natural (e.g., “exterior painting Edmonton”).
- **Performance**: optimize images, prioritize LCP, avoid heavy third‑party scripts.

## Metrics to Track (KPIs)

- **CTA CTR**: % of visitors who click primary CTAs (quote/call).
- **Conversion Rate (CVR)**: % of sessions that produce a form submit or call.
- **Call Clicks**: tap‑to‑call events from mobile/desktop.
- **Form Submissions**: completed quote/contact forms.
- **Search CTR**: Google Search Console click‑through rate for Edmonton queries.
- **Engagement**: average engagement time on page (landing page).
